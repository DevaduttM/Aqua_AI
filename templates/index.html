About Aqua AI

Welcome to AquaAI, where we harness the power of machine learning to predict the suitability of water samples for sustaining aquatic life. Our goal is to contribute towards achieving the following Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs):

1. Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation - Ensuring availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.
2. Goal 14: Life Below Water- Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources for sustainable development.

Our innovative model ensures the quality of water crucial for the health of aquatic beings, including safeguarding the water quality in aquariums. Join us in safeguarding aquatic ecosystems and promoting sustainable practices through data-driven insights.
